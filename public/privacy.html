Privacy Policy

Effective: April 21, 2026

English العربية

This Privacy Policy explains how Waya (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Waya Merchant mobile app, the Waya customer loyalty passes, and related services (together, the “Service”). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Who we are

Waya operates a digital loyalty platform used by merchants in Saudi Arabia to manage customer rewards via Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes. Questions about this Policy can be sent to privacy@trywaya.com.

2. Data we collect

2.1 From merchants using the Waya Merchant app

  • Account identifiers: email address, password (stored only as a salted hash), and Supabase-generated user ID.
  • Shop/business information you enter: shop name, phone number, branding, and loyalty program configuration.
  • Loyalty activity you record: customer points, stamps, and reward redemptions you submit using the app.
  • Device and usage information: app version, iOS/Android version, anonymous crash reports.

2.2 From customers enrolled in a merchant’s loyalty program

  • Name and mobile phone number (Saudi format), provided by the customer when they enroll for an Apple/Google Wallet loyalty pass.
  • Loyalty balance and redemption history for that merchant only.

We do not collect payment card numbers, government ID numbers, or location data.

3. How we use data

  • To create and operate your merchant account and your customers’ loyalty passes.
  • To push loyalty balance updates to each customer’s Apple Wallet or Google Wallet in real time when you record a transaction.
  • To secure the Service (rate limiting, abuse prevention, authentication).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability using aggregated, non-identifying telemetry.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use personal data for advertising.

4. How data is shared

  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider. Data is stored in Supabase-managed infrastructure under our account, protected by row-level security.
  • Apple — for APNs push notifications that update Apple Wallet passes when a customer’s balance changes. Only the pass serial number and the update signal are transmitted to Apple.
  • Google — for Google Wallet Object API updates, using only the Google Wallet object ID and the balance delta.

We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party not listed above.

5. Where data is stored

Data is stored in Supabase-managed data centers. Transport is always encrypted using TLS 1.2+. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes and are never visible to us or anyone else.

6. Retention and deletion

We keep data for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your Waya merchant account at any time from the Waya Merchant app: open the Profile screen and tap “Delete account”. Deletion is immediate and removes your shop, customers, and all loyalty history. Backups are purged within 30 days.

Customers who want their loyalty pass removed can contact the issuing merchant or email privacy@trywaya.com.

7. Your rights

  • Access — you can view the data we hold about you directly in the app.
  • Correction — update your shop info from the app.
  • Deletion — delete your account in-app (see §6).
  • Complaint — contact the Saudi Data & AI Authority (SDAIA) if you believe your rights under KSA Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) have been violated.

8. Children

The Waya Merchant app is intended for business owners aged 18+. It is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Changes to this Policy

If we make a material change to this Policy we will update the Effective date at the top of this page and, where practical, notify merchants in the app or by email before the change takes effect.

10. Contact

Email: privacy@trywaya.com
Website: https://trywaya.com

٢.١ من التجار

  • البريد الإلكتروني وكلمة المرور (مخزّنة كقيمة هاش مع ملح) ومعرّف المستخدم.

  • لإنشاء وتشغيل حسابك كتاجر وبطاقات عملائك.

لا نشارك بياناتك مع معلنين أو وسطاء بيانات أو أي جهة غير مذكورة أعلاه.

تُخزّن البيانات في مراكز بيانات Supabase. جميع الاتصالات مشفّرة بـ TLS 1.2 أو أحدث. كلمات المرور مخزّنة كقيم هاش فقط.
